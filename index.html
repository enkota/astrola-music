About

Astrola, the musical project of Scott Ramsay from Glasgow, Scotland, creates cinematic, chilled melodies that invite listeners to dive into immersive soundscapes. Inspired by artists like Ben Böhmer, Lane 8, Marsh, and Sultan + Shepard, his music blends emotive depth with melodic electronic elements.

Astrola's debut album "In These Moments" (April 26, 2024) reflects his journey in the world of melodic deep house and the second album "Time", released on July 18, 2025, featuring tracks like "Particles" and "Haze," - expanding his signature sound further.

Drawing influence from the stunning landscapes of Scotland, Astrola's work embodies a unique fusion of melodic traditions, marking his place in the evolving electronic scene.
